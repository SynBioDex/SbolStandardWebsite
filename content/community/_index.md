SBOL is an open community of synthetic biologists, software engineers, and industry professionals from around the world. The community defines the SBOL standard, builds supporting tools, and works together to make synthetic biology more reproducible and interoperable.

[Meet the SBOL leadership →](/community-leadership/)

## Related Standards Communities

Many of our members are also active in related standards initiatives:

- **SBML** — Systems Biology Markup Language ([sbml.org](https://sbml.org))
- **SBGN** — Systems Biology Graphical Notation ([sbgn.org](https://sbgn.org))
- **COMBINE** — Computational Modeling in Biology Network ([co.mbine.org](https://co.mbine.org))
