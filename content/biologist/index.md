## Focus on your Science, We'll Handle the Data
The Synthetic Biology Open Language (SBOL) isn't just for software developers. It's a standard that helps **biologists** like you accurately represent, share, and publish your genetic designs. By using SBOL-enabled tools, you can ensure your data is unambiguous, reproducible, and easy to visualize.

---
