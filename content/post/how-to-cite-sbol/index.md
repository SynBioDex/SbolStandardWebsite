If you use SBOL in your research, there are a few key papers to cite depending on which aspects of the standard you use.

## SBOL Data Model

For the current SBOL3 data model:

> McLaughlin, J.A., et al. (2020). **Synthetic biology open language (SBOL) version 3.0.0.** *Journal of Integrative Bioinformatics*, 17(2-3). https://doi.org/10.1515/jib-2020-0017

For SBOL2 (if you are using SBOL2-based tools):

> Roehner, N., et al. (2016). **Sharing structure and function in biological design with SBOL 2.0.** *ACS Synthetic Biology*, 5(6), 498–506.

## SBOL Visual

For the SBOL Visual notation:

> Beal, J., et al. (2019). **SBOL Visual 2.0.** *ACS Synthetic Biology*, 8(8), 1842-1851.

## The SBOL Standard (general)

For citing the SBOL effort broadly:

> Galdzicki, M., et al. (2014). **The Synthetic Biology Open Language (SBOL) provides a community standard for communicating designs in synthetic biology.** *Nature Biotechnology*, 32, 545–550.
