We're pleased to announce a refreshed SBOL website, designed to make it easier for everyone — biologists, developers, and newcomers — to find what they need.

## What's new

**Clearer entry points.** The new homepage guides you to the right resources depending on who you are. Not sure where to start? Choose your path:

- **Biologists**: learn how to use SBOL Visual and SynBioHub to visualize and share your designs
- **Developers**: access libraries, specifications, and online tools
- **Community members**: find out how to join Slack, attend HARMONY, COMBINE, or IWBDA, and contribute to the standard

**Simplified navigation.** The menu has been reorganized into five sections: *Learn SBOL*, *Specifications*, *Tools & Resources*, *Community*, and *Publications*. Everything is still there — it's just easier to find.

**Meet the leadership.** The new [Community Leadership](/community-leadership/) page lists the current Chair, Editors, and Steering Committee members.

**AI resources.** We've added a dedicated [AI Resources](/ai-resources/) page listing AI tools configured with SBOL knowledge, including the SBOL3 and SBOL2 ChatGPT assistants.

## Feedback welcome

This is a living site. If you spot something missing, outdated, or broken, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/SynBioDex/SbolStandardWebsite/issues) or post in the [SBOL Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/sbol-standard/shared_invite/zt-1ukcka8b8-Z4QoyiE_LwwoRBw2DzheSg).
