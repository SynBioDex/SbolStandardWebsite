Support SBOL

SBOL is a free, open standard maintained by a global volunteer community. Supporting the Bio-Design Automation Consortium (BDAC) is how you help keep SBOL active, visible, and growing.

Support via BDAC

SBOL is open and community-powered

An international standard

SBOL is the globally recognized data standard for synthetic biology, used by researchers and engineers across academia and industry to share and exchange biological designs.

Volunteer-driven

The SBOL community is a volunteer organization. There are no membership fees and participation is open to all. The standard remains free and open by design.

Staying visible takes effort

Keeping SBOL present at conferences, active in publications, and relevant to tool developers requires sustained organizational support from the broader community.

How sponsorship works

Sponsorship for SBOL flows through the Bio-Design Automation Consortium (BDAC). BDAC is the umbrella organization for the biodesign automation community, and supporting BDAC is supporting SBOL.

What your support means for SBOL

Logo in Presentations

Your organization's logo will appear in all SBOL presentations at major workshops and conferences, including IWBDA, COMBINE, and HARMONY.

Website Recognition

Your logo will be featured on the SBOL website as a recognized supporter of the standard.

Community Announcements

Recognition in SBOL community communications, including our mailing list and Slack workspace.

Steering Committee Visibility

Supporters are invited to attend SBOL Steering Committee meetings as observers, with direct visibility into the direction of the standard.

Organizations that have supported SBOL

These organizations previously supported SBOL through the SBOL Industrial Consortium. We are grateful for their contributions to the community.
